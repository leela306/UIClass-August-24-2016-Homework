Visit UCM

Welcome! We invite you, your family, and friends to visit the University of Central Missouri and the city of Warrensburg to learn about our campus and student life. Take a campus tour, meet with faculty and students, and learn why over 14,000 students call UCM home. Choose the best option below for you. We look forward to you visiting our campus soon!

Schedule a personilized campus

Tailor a visit to meet your individual needs, take a student lead campus tour, meet with your academic major and meet one on one with an Admissions Representative.

Register for an Oncampus Visit program

Join us for one of our days designed specifically for visiting students and their parents. In most cases you'll have an opportunity to meet faculty members, learn about admissions, visit a residence hall and find out about financial aid.

Questions, contact the Campus Visit Office at 660-543-8569 or 877-SAY-UCMO.

For GPS purposes, the address of the Ward Edwards Building is 116 W. South St. Warrensburg, MO 64093.

If you are interested in scheduling a tour for a group of 10 or more students, please view the group tour guidelines prior to arranging your visit.
