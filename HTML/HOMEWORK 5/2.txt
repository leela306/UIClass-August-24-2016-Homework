2) float in css?

answer:

The float property specifies whether or not a box (an element) should float.
we have float to move the elements or images to  top,left,right,bottom.

1.foalt:top
it will move the element from the top.

2.float:left
it will place the elements at the left side.This float:left will be very useful in designing of the navigation bars.

3.float:right
it will place the elements  at the right.this float:right will be very useful in designing of the navigation bars where one anchor tag place at the right top most corner.

4.float:bottom
it will place the elements  at the top.this float:left will be very useful in designing of the navigation bars.
